Nickel-Iron Heart


Drifting nowhere
Twenty-Five Kilometres in a second.


Secluded in your space
Until you met another.


Knocked you from the periodic
Fall in every direction.


Warmer than you thought possible
An unyielding path towards me.


Your burning reveals your core
Our crash obliterates two city blocks.


Notes:

I had fun with this one just describing a game of Mysterium with friends but leaving the reader with clues rather than stating it was just a board game.
Experiments here were based on setting tone and mood. Tried out the nonsense metaphor and also had to contain all the text within a short word limit.
